About Christian

A Journey Built on Excellence

Christian Fanfelle's passion for golf began at age 10, setting the foundation for a career defined by dedication and precision. After being homeschooled through high school, Christian earned a full-ride scholarship to the University of Texas at San Antonio (UTSA), where he excelled both on the course and in the classroom.

A four-time All-American Scholar and three-time CUSA All-Team selection, Christian graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Business Management and later earned an MBA. These academic achievements reflect the same discipline and work ethic that define his competitive game—disciplined iron play, strategic course management, and an unwavering commitment to excellence.

Currently competing on the All Pro Tour (APT), Christian is focused on competing at the highest levels of professional golf. Every tournament, every practice, and every round is a step toward his ultimate goal: the PGA Tour.
